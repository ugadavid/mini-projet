Participatory English Design Project – FAQ

🧠 What’s the idea?

You create a short learning activity in English for your peers — and they do the same for you. We explore how creating content helps you learn better.

🪜 How does it work?

  1. Kick-off session
    - Choose a topic (e.g., job interviews, daily routines, environment…)
    - Learn to use simple tools (H5P, LearningApps…)
    - Receive a step-by-step guide in English
  2. Creation phase
    - In pairs, build an activity for another pair
    - Be creative, keep it short and clear
  3. Exchange and feedback
    - Test each other's work
    - Give constructive feedback in English

🎯 What’s the goal?

- Learn by designing
- Reflect on what makes a good activity
- Improve your English and your digital skills

⏳ How much time will it take?

Around 3 to 4 hours in total, spread over a few weeks. One short meeting + flexible time for creation and feedback.

💡 What do I need?

- A laptop or computer
- Motivation and curiosity
- No technical skills required — we keep it simple!
